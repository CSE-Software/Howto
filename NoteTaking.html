Take Note

View project on GitHub
Note taking is the recording of information in a succinct but effective way. It helps students in learning materials or reviewing them later on.

Benefits of note-taking

  • Improve in focusing and paying attention
  • Keep students engaged in lectures
  • Store important information for reviewing later.
  • Learn to prioritize important details
  • Enhance organization skills
  • Promote creativity

How to take note

Do the following steps to have a good notes:
  • Prepared carefully before coming to class
  • Improve your listening skill and writing skill
  • Use appropriate note taking methods
  • Make your note easy to read with highlights and comments
  • Pay attention to small details
  • Review your note frequently
